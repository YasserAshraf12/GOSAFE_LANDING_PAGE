Our Problem

There are numerous types of defects that can form on our roads including deep potholes, shallow potholes, surface deterioration, edge failure, cracking, crazing, rutting, and subsidence. We believe that road deterioration problem is negatively afflicting countries all over the world. Specially in Egypt, road accidents increase significantly because of road surface defects that are widely spread among the entire road network. Moreover, the traditional manual visual road inspection process is really exhaustive and slows down the road infrastructure maintenance process that is urgently needed to ensure that the requirements for road safety, efficiency and durability are satisfied to save citizens life and to make a nation grow and develop. Road authorities suffer a lot from the considerable time, labour, and cost required for the process of human manual inspection and the action of road repairs due to the lack of road condition information.

About GOSAFE

GoSafe is autonomous detection, classification, and localization of road defects

We propose a state-of-the-art, sophisticated, and efficient solution to automate the road inspection and evaluation process overcoming the limitations of the traditional human-based onsite visual inspection.

Our Technology

Our proposed solution is a computer vision-based system for real-time detection and localization of road surface defects. The system should be attached into a car to automatically collect road defects information and is connected to a cloud database where the image with the detected defect is stored along with its type and precise location. Our main aim is to provide the road authorities with a road damage database which can be accessed and managed easily through our website to alleviate some of the difficulties they face during the road network development boosting the road infrastructure maintenance process.
